Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Spendly collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, session duration).

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the expense tracking features of the Service.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the Service.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — for typography; subject to Google's privacy policy.

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media trackers.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies solely to keep you logged in. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this will prevent you from staying signed in.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app.
